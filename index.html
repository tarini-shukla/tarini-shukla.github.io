I am Tarini Shukla,

PhD student in UNC Charlotte, NC, USA

My research interest is in the study of microtopography utilizing high-resolution remote sensing technology and GIS.

Serve as a lead researcher with US Forest Services and USDA in Santee Experimental Forest; use terrestrial lidar, aerial lidar, and unmanned aerial systems (UAS) to build analytics, numerical and simulation models for impact quantification and qualitative characterization of microtopography on carbon dynamics in tidal freshwater forested wetlands through biogeochemical modeling.
